Target and rules

The game «Minesweeper» starts when a player clicks on a cell with the left mouse button. The first click is always safe and reveals a number or an opening surrounded by numbers. Each number tells you how many mines touch the square. You can mark a mine by putting a flag on it with the right mouse button. If all the mines touching a number are flagged, you can press both buttons on that number to clear the remaining adjacent cells. You win by clearing all the safe cells and lose if you click on a mine. A mine counter tells you how many mines are still hidden and a time counter keeps track of your score.

Game field

The game «Minesweeper» have three levels of difficulty: Beginner, Intermediate, Expert.

Beginner has 81 cells and 10 mines

Intermediate has 256 cells and 40 mines

Expert has 512 cells and 99 mines

To make the game field of any size, go to menu Game and click Preferences. Game «Minesweeper» supports fields by size up to 512 cells and up to 99 mines.
